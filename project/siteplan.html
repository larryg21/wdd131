Toronto Evening Pacers

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Toronto Evening Pacers

This name represents a community of working professionals in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) who gather for evening runs, fitness, and social activities after work hours. The name captures the friendly and motivating spirit of the group.

Optional domain: torontoeveningpacers.ca

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a central hub for the Toronto Evening Pacers running group. The website will share the weekly running schedule, routes, community updates, and an easy way for new members to join. It will also promote the health benefits of group running and encourage social connection among participants in Toronto.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the next evening run location and meeting time?
  • How do I join the Toronto Evening Pacers and learn about membership guidelines?
  • Are there beginner-friendly or short-distance routes available for new runners?

Color Schema

The selected colors convey energy, reliability, and motivation, matching the spirit of group running and evening activity.

  • Primary: Deep Blue (#1e3a8a) – used for headers and navigation.
  • Accent: Bright Orange (#f97316) – used for buttons and highlights.
  • Background: Light Gray (#f9fafb) – for overall page background.

Typography

  • Headings: Playfair Display – clean serif font for titles and headings.
  • Body Text: Roboto – modern sans-serif font for readable content.
